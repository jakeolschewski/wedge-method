## The 5-Section AI Consulting Proposal Template

Every winning consulting proposal follows this structure. Copy this template, then use the AI prompts below to fill each section.

### Section 1: Executive Summary

**Purpose:** Hook the client in 3–4 sentences. State the problem, your solution, and the outcome.

**AI Prompt:**
```
Write a 3-sentence executive summary for a consulting proposal. 
The client is [COMPANY NAME], a [INDUSTRY] company with [SIZE] employees. 
Their main challenge is [PROBLEM]. My proposed solution is [SOLUTION]. 
The expected outcome is [OUTCOME]. Write in a confident, direct tone.
```

---

### Section 2: Problem Statement

**Purpose:** Show the client you understand their situation better than they do.

**AI Prompt:**
```
Write a problem statement for a consulting proposal. 
The client is experiencing [PROBLEM]. The business impact of this problem is [IMPACT].
If left unaddressed, the consequences will be [CONSEQUENCES].
Write 2–3 paragraphs. Be specific. Avoid generic consulting language.
```

---

### Section 3: Proposed Approach

**Purpose:** Describe your methodology in phases. Be specific enough to build trust, vague enough to protect your IP.

**AI Prompt:**
```
Write a 3-phase consulting approach for solving [PROBLEM].
Phase 1: Discovery and assessment (2 weeks)
Phase 2: Solution design and recommendations (3 weeks)  
Phase 3: Implementation support and knowledge transfer (4 weeks)
Include 2–3 deliverables per phase. Write in professional consulting language.
```

---

### Section 4: Investment & Pricing

**Purpose:** Present pricing with confidence. Anchor high, justify with ROI.

**AI Prompt:**
```
Write a consulting pricing section for a proposal.
My fee for this engagement is $[AMOUNT].
The ROI for the client is [ROI CALCULATION].
Payment terms: 50% upfront, 50% on completion.
Write 2 paragraphs that frame the investment as an ROI decision, not a cost.
```

---

### Section 5: About & Credibility

**Purpose:** Why you, why now. Keep it tight — 1 paragraph max.

**AI Prompt:**
```
Write a 1-paragraph "About the Consultant" section for a proposal.
My name is [NAME]. I specialize in [SPECIALTY].
I've helped [NUMBER] clients achieve [OUTCOME].
Notable past clients or results: [EXAMPLES].
Write in third person. Be confident, not boastful.
```

## 3 Proposal Templates for Common Consulting Engagements

### Template A: Strategy Consulting Proposal
Best for: Market entry, growth strategy, competitive analysis
- Discovery phase: Stakeholder interviews + data review
- Deliverable: 30-slide strategy deck + implementation roadmap
- Timeline: 6–8 weeks
- Typical range: $15,000–$50,000

### Template B: Technology Implementation Consulting
Best for: Software selection, digital transformation, systems integration
- Discovery phase: Tech stack audit + requirements gathering
- Deliverable: Vendor comparison matrix + implementation plan
- Timeline: 4–12 weeks
- Typical range: $20,000–$100,000+

### Template C: Fractional CMO / Advisor Retainer
Best for: Ongoing strategic guidance, monthly advisory relationships
- Format: Monthly retainer with defined hours and deliverables
- Deliverable: Monthly strategy sessions + async availability
- Timeline: 3–12 month engagement
- Typical range: $3,000–$15,000/month

## Tips for Getting the Best Output From AI Proposal Templates

1. **Give context upfront**: The more you tell the AI about the client and project, the better the output.
2. **Edit for voice**: AI drafts sound generic. Spend 15 minutes adding your specific stories and phrasing.
3. **Never copy-paste directly**: Clients can tell. Use AI as a first draft, not a final draft.
4. **Build a library**: Save your best proposals. Use them as examples in future AI prompts.
5. **Systematize, don't just automate**: The goal is a repeatable process, not a one-time shortcut.
