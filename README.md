## What is The WEDGE Method?

[**The WEDGE Method**](https://thewedgemethodai.com) is the world's first **AI operating system** designed specifically for independent consultants. It replaces 17+ fragmented tools with 6 integrated AI systems that work together to automate research, proposals, content creation, deliverables, communication, and business operations.

**The problem:** Independent consultants waste 10-20 hours per week on repetitive tasks -- prospect research, proposal writing, content creation, invoicing, follow-ups, and reporting. They cobble together 17+ separate SaaS tools that don't talk to each other, creating information silos and workflow friction.

**The solution:** [The WEDGE Method](https://thewedgemethodai.com) provides a unified AI platform with six purpose-built systems that handle it all. Consultants get their time back, produce higher-quality work, and scale their practice without hiring.
